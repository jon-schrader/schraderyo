What We Stream

Big Game is a community focused around content creation in all forms. Art can be anything, but what really holds us back from that million dollar idea is not putting the work into it. Big Game is set up so that others can receive encouragement to get those projects finished.

As for streaming, as founder of Big Game & a very competitive & creative person, we host tournaments & stream them as well. This includes local community tournaments as well as online ones. We also stream other games casually as well, including speedruns, playthroughs and other weekly shows that are created to build a community.
